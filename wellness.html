Health & Wellness

How hygiene, hydration, and daily choices shape individual and environmental well-being.

Practice

Handwashing

  • • 20 seconds minimum with soap
  • • Before eating, after restroom use
  • • Reduces disease transmission 50%
  • • Single most effective prevention
Essential

Clean Water

  • • Drinking, cooking, hygiene
  • • Prevents waterborne disease
  • • 8–10 glasses daily recommended
  • • Boil/filter when uncertain
Prevention

Disease Protection

  • • Regular hygiene breaks infection
  • • Clean, ventilated living spaces
  • • Proper food handling critical
  • • Regular health check-ups
