Water Conservation Tips

Shower

Reduce shower time to 5 minutes. Install low-flow showerheads (4L/min vs. 9.5L/min).

Dishes

Run dishwasher only when full. Hand-wash efficiently with minimal running water.

Laundry

Wash clothes only when needed. Use cold water and full loads (saves 40% water).

Cooking

Reuse cooking water for plants. Use lids to reduce boiling time.
